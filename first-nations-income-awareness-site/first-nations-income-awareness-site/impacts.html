Living conditions & housing

When income is low, options shrink: repairs get delayed, safe alternatives are limited, and stable housing becomes harder to secure.

Low income can mean harder travel to care, fewer options for nutritious food, and more stress. Colonial harms also created intergenerational trauma that affects health.
